# Roadmap

## 1.0 baseline

- Stable guided and direct rolling workflow.
- Persistent combinations, history, settings, and eight accent colors.
- Click support on every Cardputer and optional motion support when an IMU is available.
- English and Brazilian Portuguese interfaces.

## After 1.0

- Refined hand-drawn sprite atlas.
- Dedicated Cardputer ADV motion calibration profiles.
- Keep/drop highest and lowest dice.
- Exploding dice and configurable success pools.
- Character and campaign profiles.
- Import/export through microSD.
- Optional wireless sharing and VTT integrations.

The core roller will remain fast and system-agnostic as optional rules grow around it.
