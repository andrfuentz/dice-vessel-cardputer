# DICE\\VESSEL v1.0.0

**KEEP ROLLING.**

DICE\\VESSEL is a pocket RPG campaign companion for the M5Stack Cardputer. Build a roll in a few guided steps, reuse named combinations, revisit recent results, and roll with a key or—when an IMU is available—a shake.

## Highlights

- Guided builder and direct mixed-dice expressions with final bonuses or penalties.
- D2, D4, D6, D8, D10, D12, D20, and percentile D100.
- Normal, Advantage, and Disadvantage modes for single-D20 checks.
- Click-to-roll on every Cardputer and optional shake-to-roll.
- Cinematic collision-based animation and responsive wooden-box audio.
- Eight named saved combinations and persistent ten-roll history.
- English and Brazilian Portuguese interfaces.
- Eight persistent accent colors with the original black-and-Amber style as default.
- Animated charging screen and on-device instructions.

Natural 20 and Natural 1 presentation is intentionally limited to single-D20 checks, including modifiers, Advantage, and Disadvantage. Mixed expressions and dice pools remain system-agnostic.

## Download

- Fresh installation: `dicevessel-v1.0.0-factory.bin` at offset `0x0`.
- Update preserving settings and saved data: `dicevessel-v1.0.0-firmware.bin` at offset `0x10000`.

See the README and flashing guide for controls, requirements, and installation details.
